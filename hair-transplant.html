Hair Transplant FAQ

Your Questions, Answered

Is a hair transplant permanent?
Yes. Transplanted follicles are taken from areas resistant to balding, so they continue to grow naturally for life once they settle.
What is the difference between FUE and DHI?
FUE extracts individual follicles with no linear scar and faster recovery. DHI offers precise graft placement for a denser, natural-looking hairline with minimal downtime. We'll recommend the right one for you.
Is the procedure painful?
It's performed under local anaesthesia, so the procedure itself is comfortable. Most patients return to routine activities within a day or two.
How much does a hair transplant cost in Nellore?
Cost depends on the number of grafts and technique. You'll receive a clear, upfront estimate during your free consultation — no hidden charges.
When will I see results?
Initial growth begins around 3–4 months, with fuller, natural-looking results typically by 8–12 months.
