Skin Care FAQ

Your Questions, Answered

How many sessions will I need for acne?
It depends on the type and severity. After your free skin analysis, the doctor will suggest a clear plan — many patients see noticeable improvement within a few sessions.
Is glutathione whitening safe?
When done under medical supervision with proper dosing, glutathione treatments are considered safe. Our doctors assess your skin and health first.
Is laser hair removal painful?
Modern lasers are designed for comfort, with most patients describing only a mild sensation. Multiple sessions give long-lasting reduction.
Will carbon or pico laser suit my skin?
Carbon and pico lasers are suitable for most skin types and are great for glow, pigmentation, pores and oil control. Your doctor will confirm during the consultation.
Do you offer treatments for pigmentation and dark spots?
Yes — chemical peels, lasers and targeted plans are used to fade pigmentation and even out skin tone safely.
