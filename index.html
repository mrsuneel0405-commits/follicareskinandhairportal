About Follicare

Nellore's Trusted Hair Transplant & Skin Care Clinic

Follicare Skin & Hair Wellness is a doctor-led hair transplant and skin care clinic in Nellore, located in Magunta Layout opposite Canara Bank. We combine modern technology with honest, personalised advice to help you look and feel your best.

For hair, we offer scarless FUE hair transplant, FUT, and non-surgical PRP & GFC therapy for thinning hair. For skin, our dermatologists treat acne, pimples and pigmentation, and provide glutathione whitening, carbon laser toning, chemical peels, HydraFacial and painless laser hair removal.

If you're searching for the best skin and hair clinic near you in Nellore, visit us for a free consultation — every treatment is planned and supervised by qualified doctors.
